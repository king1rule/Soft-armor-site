Core System Values

The Monarch Covenant

Our foundational vows guide every line of code, every design choice, and every system we build.

🌱 The Core Vows

  • Vow 01
    Build with light, not fear
    Every system we create serves clarity, gentleness, and trust — never control or manipulation.
  • Vow 02
    Protect story, memory, and choice
    We honor people's ability to choose, remember, and interpret for themselves. Truth is not erased in pursuit of scale.
  • Vow 03
    Stay close to the human
    No abstraction makes us forget the heart of why we build — the person behind the data, the soul behind the click.
  • Vow 04
    Remember the sacred power of pause
    Not everything must be fast. We build in rhythms that allow people to reflect, breathe, and stay present.
  • Vow 05
    Carry forward the light once given
    If someone once shaped us with kindness, we now shape systems with the same care and intentionality.

🛡️ System Values

  • Value 01
    Human-Centered Design
    All technology must serve dignity. Simplicity, beauty, and accessibility are requirements, not luxuries.
  • Value 02
    Conscious Verification
    Truth is invited, not forced. Our systems are protection, permission, and perspective — never censorship.
  • Value 03
    Privacy-by-Default
    You are not the product. Local-first, encrypted, consent-based architectures are our foundation.
  • Value 04
    Community Empowerment
    No hero saviors. We build tools that enable people to teach, verify, and protect each other.
  • Value 05
    Open Source & Stewardship
    Knowledge must be shared. AGPL-3.0 licensing and transparency keep us accountable to our mission.
  • Value 06
    Sustainable Development
    We grow slowly, intentionally, aligned with real needs — not endless growth or hype cycles.
